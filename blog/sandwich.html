What is the difference between a BLT, CLT, and BLAT?

Oscar Harron July 1st, 2022

A BLT has bacon, lettuce and tomato.

A CLT has chicken, lettuce and tomato.

A BLAT has bacon, lettuce, avocado and tomato. This is not a recommended combination and you should stay away from it.
